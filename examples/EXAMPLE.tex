% rat latex-unicode comprehensive demonstration

This file demonstrates text commands, inline math, and display math.

\textbf{Bold text} / \textit{Italic text} / \emph{Emphasized text}

Inline math with $...$:
$E=mc^2$, $\alpha + \beta \to \gamma$, $\sum_{k=1}^{n} k$

Inline math with \(...\):
\(\int_0^1 x^2\,dx\), \(\frac{a}{b}\), \(\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\)

Display math with $$...$$:
$$
\frac{1}{2} + \sum_{k=1}^{n} k
$$

Display math with \[...\]:
\[
\left(\frac{a+b}{c}\right)^2 = \frac{a^2 + 2ab + b^2}{c^2}
\]

Aligned environment:
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= x^2 + 2x + 1 \\
g(x) &= \frac{1}{x} + \sqrt{x}
\end{aligned}

Align environment:
\begin{align}
a &= b + c \\
d &= \frac{1}{2}
\end{align}

Align* environment:
\begin{align*}
\nabla \cdot \vec{E} &= \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0} \\
\nabla \times \vec{B} &= \mu_0 \vec{J}
\end{align*}

Mixed paragraph:
When \textbf{style} and math are combined, for example $x^2$ and \(\beta\),
output should remain plain Unicode text.
